🧠 Who Am I?

I'm Wyatt—builder, breaker, automator. I spend most of my time navigating the weird intersection of IT ops, app dev, and creative problem-solving. This site? It started as a Flask sandbox, but it's grown into a place where I tinker, test ideas, and experiment with making tech that actually *feels good* to use.

I like figuring things out the hard way, not because it’s efficient—but because it teaches me something real. That’s the core of this whole setup. Controlled chaos, but it works.
